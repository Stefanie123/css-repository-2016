Vindinium

What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is an Al (Artificial Intelligence) challenge program made of coding. The objective of the challenge is to gain more gold than the other bots. The bots are the players. By making different functions for your bot, such as finding taverns and attacking when your bot has a certain number of health, the coding allows your bot to do specific actions. At the end of the challenge, whichever bot has the most number of gold wins.
